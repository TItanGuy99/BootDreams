DiscJuggler images

BootDreams is capable of creating audio/data and data/data DiscJuggler 4.0 images using cdi4dc.

There is also support for burning most DiscJuggler images. If you have an image that is in the data/data format and it has CDDA tracks then the image may not fit on the disc because an extra 150 sectors are used between each track burned. Additionally images with more than two sessions has not been tested, but may or may not burn, depending on the image's layout.
